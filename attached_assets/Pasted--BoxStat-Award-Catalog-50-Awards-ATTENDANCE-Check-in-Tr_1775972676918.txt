## Icon Notes

- All icons are Lucide React component names ‚Äî import via `import { CircleCheck, Footprints, ... } from 'lucide-react'`
- `Flame` is used twice (#14 Warming Up, #15 On Fire) ‚Äî tier background differentiates them
- `Crown` is used twice (#9 Franchise Player, #29 OG) ‚Äî different categories make them distinct
- Icons render as white strokes centered on the tier badge PNG placeholder
- Recommended `strokeWidth={1.8}` and `size={badgeSize * 0.38}` for consistent appearance

## Progression Notes

- **Attendance chain** (9 deep) is the longest ‚Äî rewards the behavior coaches care about most
- **Streak chain** jumps from Bronze straight to Gold ‚Äî streaks are hard, the reward should reflect that
- **Stats chain** has a single-stat track (On the Board ‚Üí Stat Machine) and a parallel multi-stat track (Well Rounded ‚Üí All-Round Legend) ‚Äî coaches configure which stats matter for their sport
- **Manual awards** have no prerequisites ‚Äî coaches should be free to recognize anyone at any level
- **Collection awards** create a meta-game: earning awards earns more awards
- **The Collector** was bumped from "any 5" to "any 10" to keep Diamond feeling earned
