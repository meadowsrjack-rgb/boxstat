Badges
Hall of Famer (Yellow)
This tier represents the most elite achievements, requiring exceptional dedication, skill, and long-term commitment.
Superstar (10× MVP): For demonstrating a consistent pattern of dominance.
Prime Time (10× Clutch): For being known to deliver in the biggest moments.
Force of Will (10× Comeback): For an unbreakable spirit that refuses to lose.
Relentless (10× Hustle): For a motor that never stops.
The Linchpin (10× Teammate): For being absolutely essential to team cohesion.
Spirit of the Game (10× Sportsmanship): For embodying the highest ideals of fair play.
Coach on the Court (10× Student): For understanding the game on a deeper, strategic level.
The Paragon (10× Lead by Example): For being the ultimate embodiment of how to prepare and play.
Coach's Choice: For earning each of the eight core coach awards at least once.
The Trifecta: For earning an MVP, Hustle, and Teammate award in the same season.
Character Captain: For earning a Hustle, Teammate, and Sportsmanship award in the same season.
Practice Legend: For attending 250 total team practices.
Skills Virtuoso: For attending 250 total skills sessions.
Franchise Player: For playing in 250 total games.
FNH Hall of Famer: For playing in 250 total FNH games.
Dynasty Member: For being an active member of the team for five full years.
Online Foundation Program:
The Digital Triple Crown: Awarded for completing all Skill, all Strength & Conditioning, and all Basketball IQ videos in the Foundation Program. This requires earning the "Technique Titan," "Peak Performer," and "Basketball Savant" badges.
Foundation Alumnus: For completing the entire 12-week Foundation Program a second time, demonstrating an elite commitment to mastering the fundamentals.
Superstar (Purple)
This tier is for impressive milestones, season-long consistency, and advanced collector achievements.
Marquee Player (5× MVP): For being a recognized star and reliable top performer.
Ice in the Veins (5× Clutch): For thriving under pressure.
Momentum Shifter (5× Comeback): For changing the tone of a game.
The Workhorse (5× Hustle): For having an elite work ethic.
The Cornerstone (5× Teammate): For being a player the team is built around emotionally.
The Ambassador (5× Sportsmanship): For being a positive representative of the team.
The Tactician (5× Student): For a high in-game IQ.
The Blueprint (5× Lead by Example): For providing a model for others to follow.
The Pillar: For attending every single scheduled practice in a season.
Ironman: For playing in every single game of a season.
FNH Ironman: For playing in every FNH game in a single season.
Practice Centurion: For attending 100 total team practices.
Skills Specialist: For attending 100 total skills sessions.
Century Club: For playing in 100 total games.
FNH Legend: For playing in 100 total FNH games.
Team Veteran: For being an active member of the team for three full years.
Online Foundation Program:
Foundation Graduate: The premier online award for completing all 60 videos in the 12-week Foundation Program.
Technique Titan: Awarded for completing all 36 Skill videos in the program.
Peak Performer: Awarded for completing all 12 Strength & Conditioning videos in the program.
Basketball Savant: Awarded for completing all 12 Basketball IQ videos in the program.
Perfect Attendance: Online: For completing all required videos each week for 12 consecutive weeks without missing a week.
All-Star (Blue)
This tier recognizes significant commitment, mid-level milestones, and the first level of collector badges.
Game Changer (3× MVP): For consistently making a pivotal impact.
The Closer (3× Clutch): For making multiple high-pressure plays.
The Sparkplug (3× Comeback): For consistently igniting team rallies.
The Engine (3× Hustle): For being a consistent source of energy.
The Glue (3× Teammate): For being a positive and unifying presence.
Class Act (3× Sportsmanship): For consistent respectful conduct.
The Protégé (3× Student): For being exceptionally coachable and attentive.
The Standard (3× Lead by Example): For setting the bar for effort and execution.
The Dependable: For submitting a timely RSVP for every event in a season.
Recruiter: Awarded for referring a new player who joins.
Practice Fiend: For attending 50 total practices.
Skills Devotee: For attending 50 total skills sessions.
Seasoned Competitor: For playing in 50 total games.
FNH Veteran: For playing in 50 total FNH games.
First Year Anniversary: For being an active member of the team for one full year.
Online Foundation Program:
Half-Time Adjustments: For completing 30 total videos in the Foundation Program, marking the halfway point.
Monthly Module Master: For completing all assigned videos for four consecutive weeks.
Skills Sharp-Shooter: For completing 20 different Skill videos.
Conditioning Core: For completing 6 different Strength & Conditioning videos.
Strategic Specialist: For completing 6 different Basketball IQ videos.
Mid-Season Mentor: For completing all weekly videos for 6 consecutive weeks.

This tier is for demonstrating consistent habits and achieving early milestones.
Locked In: For attending 10 consecutive scheduled practices.
On the Ball: For submitting a timely RSVP for 10 consecutive events.
Tournament Titan: For checking into all games in a single tournament.
Dedicated Grinder: For attending 25 total practices.
Skills Seeker: For attending 25 total skills sessions.
Regular Competitor: For playing in 25 total games.
FNH Regular: For playing in 25 total FNH games.

Sportsmanship Award: Display of outstanding sportsmanship.
Hustle Award: Relentless hustle and effort.
Teammate Award: Encouraging, supportive, or unselfish play.
Student of the Game: Exceptional listening skills and attentiveness.
Lead by Example: For flawlessly demonstrating a drill or skill.
Online Foundation Program:
Weekly Warm-Up: Awarded for completing all 5 assigned videos in a single week (3 Skill, 1 S&C, 1 IQ).
Film Study: For completing 10 total online training videos.
Skill Builder: For completing 5 different Skill videos.
Foundation Force: For completing 5 different Strength & Conditioning videos.
Court Visionary: For completing 5 different Basketball IQ videos.
Back-to-Back Sessions: For completing all weekly videos two weeks in a row.
Prospect (Grey)
This tier is for introductory, first-time achievements that every player can earn.
Checked In: For checking into any UYP event.
Road Warrior: For checking into an event at a different location.
Holiday Hero: For playing in a game on a major holiday.
The Debut: For playing in your very first game.
Friday Follower: For playing in your first Friday Night Hoops game.
Heating Up: For attending 5 consecutive scheduled practices.
Game Planner: For RSVPing to a Game within its proper window.
Practice Planner: For RSVPing to a Practice, Skill, or FNH event.
First Reps: For attending 10 total practices.
Skill Starter: For attending 10 total skills sessions.
First Ten: For playing in 10 total games.
FNH Fighter: For playing in 10 total FNH games.
Online Foundation Program:
Digital First Steps: For watching your very first video in the Online Foundation Program.
Virtual Handles: For completing your first Skill video.
Remote Strength: For completing your first Strength & Conditioning video.
Mindful Start: For completing your first Basketball IQ video.
