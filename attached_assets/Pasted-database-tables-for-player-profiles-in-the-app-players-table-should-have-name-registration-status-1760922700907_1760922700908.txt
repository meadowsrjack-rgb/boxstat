trophies and badges: Trophies (End of Season/Year)
These trophies are awarded at the end of the year to recognize players for their outstanding season-long contributions.
UYP Legacy Trophies (Yearly)
These premier trophies represent the highest honor a player can receive. Only one recipient is selected for each award from the entire pool of players across all UYP teams, making this a truly exceptional achievement.
The UYP Heart and Hustle Award: This yearly award recognizes the single player in all of UYP who most consistently gave their all, demonstrating exceptional effort and determination in every practice and game.
The Spirit Award: Awarded to the one player across the entire organization who best maintained a positive attitude, lifted team morale, and represented the character of UYP both on and off the court.
Team Trophies (Seasonal)
Awarded by the coach to recognize standout players within their own team.
MVP (Most Valuable Player): Awarded to the player who had the most significant impact on the team's success during the season.
Coach’s Award: Given to the player who best exemplifies the team's core values and the coach's philosophy, demonstrating coachability, dedication, and a positive attitude throughout the season.
MIP (Most Improved Player): Awarded to the player who has shown the most significant growth and development in their skills and game sense over the course of the season.
Defensive Player: Awarded to the player that has had the greatest defensive impact during the season.
