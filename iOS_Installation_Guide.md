# UYP Basketball iOS Installation Guide

Your UYP Basketball app is now ready to be installed on iOS devices as a standalone app! Here's how to install it:

## Installation Steps for iOS

### Step 1: Open Safari
- Open the UYP Basketball web app in **Safari** (not Chrome or other browsers)
- Navigate to the app URL provided by your league administrator

### Step 2: Add to Home Screen
1. Tap the **Share** button at the bottom of Safari (square with arrow pointing up)
2. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
3. You'll see the UYP Basketball icon and app name
4. Tap **"Add"** in the top right corner

### Step 3: Launch the App
- Find the UYP Basketball icon on your home screen
- Tap it to launch the app in standalone mode (no browser interface)
- The app will open just like a native iOS app!

## App Features
✅ **Standalone Experience** - Runs like a native iOS app  
✅ **Offline Support** - Works even without internet connection  
✅ **Push Notifications** - Get alerts for games and events  
✅ **Quick Access** - Launch directly from home screen  
✅ **Full Screen** - No browser bars or buttons  

## System Requirements
- iOS 11.3 or later
- Safari browser (for initial installation)
- Internet connection (for initial download)

## Troubleshooting

**Problem**: "Add to Home Screen" option doesn't appear  
**Solution**: Make sure you're using Safari browser, not Chrome or other browsers

**Problem**: App doesn't work offline  
**Solution**: Make sure you've opened the app at least once with internet connection

**Problem**: App looks different from the website  
**Solution**: This is normal! The installed app has a cleaner, native-like interface
